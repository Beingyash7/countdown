# Off-Page SEO Execution Checklist (10th SSC)

## Citation and Profile Links (20-30)
- Create branded profiles with your exact site URL on student/education communities.
- Add short profile description with natural anchors:
  - `10th SSC exam countdown`
  - `10th SSC revision plan`
  - `SSC board exam FAQ`
- Keep anchor mix mostly branded and URL anchors.

## Answer-Style Posts (5-8)
- Publish practical answers on allowed communities for:
  - `How to prepare for 10th SSC in 10 days`
  - `How to improve SSC math score`
  - `Class 10 SSC revision timetable`
- Deep link to relevant internal pages, not only homepage.

## Weekly Content Distribution
- Share one weekly update post with one deep link:
  - Week 1: `/revision-plan`
  - Week 2: `/math-plan`
  - Week 3: `/science-plan`
  - Week 4: `/english-plan`

## Guardrails
- No bulk exact-match backlinks.
- No automated link blasts.
- Avoid low-quality link farms.
